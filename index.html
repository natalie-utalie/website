About Me

I am a Cambridge-certified IB teacher with a Master's degree in pedagogy and over seven years of diverse instructional experience. Throughout my career, I have successfully prepared more than 100 students for various tests and exams. Being fluent in English and Russian, I am adept at teaching both children and adults. I'm currently enhancing my skill set with IBM's web development course, gaining programming proficiency, and I'm especially proud that I've built this entire website myself.

Just Do Speak!

Not just an English speaking club but a community of like-minded people.

Professional hosts

Three English teachers with Master's degrees to facilitate you on your journey to speak your mind.

Handmade activites

At least three carefully prepared activities, all centered around a single topic, to help you delve deeper into the theme and retain as much as possible.

Small groups

Activities are organized in pairs and groups, with a maximum of six people in each group, including a host to facilitate and encourage discussion.

Laid-back atmosphere

Stress-free environment for members to enhance their communication skills ensures that every participant can enjoy a supportive and friendly space to develop their speaking abilities.
